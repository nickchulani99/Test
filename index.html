With spring comes rejuvenation. A birth. A new life. We are aiming to breathe life back into the Cascade Club. Some changes may be small and subtle, and others larger than life, but whatever the change is, our intent is clear.
Change is inevitable. Even our bodies are in a constant state of ‘flux’ or change. The cells of the human body can regenerate themselves every 8 to 10 days for smaller cells, and then weeks, months and years for larger cells. I think it is a perfect analogy for the club. Some things can be changed quickly and seemingly overnight, while some changes may take weeks, or even months to implement.
Initially, we made changes to the lights in the reception area and corridor. Then we moved to the jacuzzi and surrounding areas for repairs in the male and female lockers.
Next, we are giving the locker rooms a facelift with new wallpaper in both the male and female locker rooms. Its often the smallest changes that have the biggest impact and as you can see from the Members Update below, we are not stopping there.
Both are equally important and yet either way, we are in a state of constant change and moving forward toward a new beginning. We hope you will continue to move forward with us.
Darren Hancock
Club Manager
